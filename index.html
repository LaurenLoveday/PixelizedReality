About Lauren Loveday

What I do

I am a Graphic Designer, I am skilled in wide array of trades, disciplines and software. I can provide Graphic Design, Illustration, Colouring, Motion Design, Web Design, Photography, Filming & Much More.

Who I am

My name is Lauren Loveday, I'm from Thunder Bay Ontario and moved to Windsor, Ontario to study Graphic Design & Journalism at St Clair College.

My philosophy

It is my goal to provide you with the skills you need to make a project come to life.
